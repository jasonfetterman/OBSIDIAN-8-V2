1. PURPOSE

Define mechanical load distribution and structural integrity for OBSIDIAN-8 V3.3  
(hybrid 3030/2020 backbone + 3D printed limbs and modules).  

Ensures:

- Frame and actuators handle static, dynamic, and impact loads  
- Reference for FEA, CM, FMEA, and deployment validation  
- Supports stair traversal, payload handling, and normal locomotion

2. LOAD TYPES

Load Type | Description | Notes
--- | --- | ---
Static Load | Weight of chassis, payload, battery, armor at rest | Base design load (~24.8 kg total)
Dynamic Load | Forces during walking, stair traversal, acceleration/deceleration | Up to 1.5× static
Impact Load | Sudden contact or collision | Safety factor ≥ 2.0
Torsional Load | Twist forces on frame due to uneven terrain | Evaluated per actuator mount
Bending Load | Beam bending on chassis and limb mounts | Monitored via FEA

3. LOAD DISTRIBUTION

- **Legs:** Each leg designed to support 35–40% of total static mass during neutral stance (reduced due to lighter 3D printed limbs)  
- **Torso Modules:** Evenly distribute battery and mission payload along X-axis to minimize torsion  
- **Armor Panels:** Lightweight printed + polycarbonate panels reduce uneven lateral loading  
- **Actuators:** Torque limits enforce safe operation under dynamic loads  
- **Support Polygon:** CG maintained within polygon defined by limb contact points to prevent tipping

4. SAFETY FACTORS AND MARGINS

- **Material Factor of Safety:** ≥ 2.0 for all primary load-bearing elements  
- **Joint Stress Limit:** 80% of yield strength under max expected load  
- **Deflection Limits:**  
  - Chassis backbone: ≤ 1.5 mm under max static load  
  - Limb mounting plates/brackets: ≤ 2 mm under max dynamic load (3D printed gussets reinforced)  

5. LOAD CASES

Case ID | Scenario | Max Load (kg) | Notes
--- | --- | --- | ---
LC-001 | Neutral stance, static | 24.8 | Base CG (V3.3 mass)
LC-002 | Forward motion, flat terrain | 38 | Includes dynamic torque
LC-003 | Stair ascent, full payload | 42 | Step height 0.3 m
LC-004 | Stair descent, full payload | 42 | Step height 0.3 m
LC-005 | Sudden impact, frontal collision | 56 | Safety factor applied  

Notes: All cases analyzed via Finite Element Analysis (FEA) to confirm stress limits, deflection, and safety margins

7. DESIGN INTENT

- Maintain structural integrity across all operational scenarios  
- Minimize deflection and stress on critical components  
- Ensure actuator and limb safety under dynamic and impact loads  
- Provide predictable mechanical behavior for control authority and reactive safety systems  
- Fully traceable and CM-compliant for all load-bearing elements  
- Supports hybrid frame and modular printed components for easy replacement and upgrade
