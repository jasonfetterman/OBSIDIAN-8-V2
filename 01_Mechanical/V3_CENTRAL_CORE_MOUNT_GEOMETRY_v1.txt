OBSIDIAN-8 V3
CENTRAL CORE MOUNT GEOMETRY v3
Based on: V3.3_HYBRID_FRAME
Status: CORE STRUCTURAL INTERFACE DEFINITION
Units: Millimeters

---------------------------------------
PURPOSE
---------------------------------------

Define central structural mounting zone
for propulsion spine, avionics core,
power system, and mission payload stack.

Core must:
- Remain within reserved clearance zone
- Tie into 3030 main ring and radial 2020 braces
- Avoid loading printed parts in tension

---------------------------------------
RESERVED CORE VOLUME
---------------------------------------

Central Clearance Cylinder:
Diameter: 300 mm
Height: Full FRAME_HEIGHT (~1143 mm)

No permanent structure allowed
outside this boundary without documented update.

---------------------------------------
CORE SUPPORT STRATEGY
---------------------------------------

Primary support via:
6 radial 2020 braces (bottom plane)

Secondary support (optional):
Upper radial tie-ins at 60–65% frame height

Core must not:
Hang solely from upper ring.

---------------------------------------
LOWER CORE PLATE
---------------------------------------

Mount Type:
Circular or hexagonal plate

Recommended Diameter:
220–260 mm

Material:
Aluminum plate (6–8 mm thick)
or composite equivalent

Mounting:
Bolted to radial 2020 braces using M6 fasteners.
Use printed gusset brackets only for lateral support, not primary load.

---------------------------------------
UPPER CORE STABILIZER
---------------------------------------

Optional upper stabilizer ring
attached to vertical posts at ~700 mm height.

Purpose:
Prevent lateral oscillation of central stack.

---------------------------------------
LOAD PATH
---------------------------------------

Core Mass
→ Lower Core Plate
→ Radial 2020 Braces
→ 3030 Ring Nodes
→ Vertical Posts

No single radial brace should carry majority of load.
Mass distribution must remain symmetrical.

---------------------------------------
MASS LIMITS
---------------------------------------

Maximum recommended central core mass:
20–25 kg

If exceeding:
Add secondary reinforcement ring or additional radial braces.

---------------------------------------
FASTENER RULES
---------------------------------------

Core-to-brace interface:
M6 Grade 10.9 minimum

Bolt spacing:
≥ 40 mm apart

No single-bolt cantilever mounts allowed.

Printed brackets may be used only for alignment and vibration damping.

---------------------------------------
VIBRATION CONTROL
---------------------------------------

Core plate may use:
Moderate vibration isolators between electronics and plate.

Do not isolate entire core mass from frame structure.

---------------------------------------
CENTER OF GRAVITY CONTROL
---------------------------------------

Heavy components (batteries):
Mount below 50% of frame height

Avoid tall, narrow mass stacks.
Centralize compute modules near geometric center.

---------------------------------------
ALIGNMENT REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------

Core plate center must align
within ±2 mm of geometric origin.

Vertical alignment tolerance:
< 1° deviation

---------------------------------------
FAILURE CONDITIONS
---------------------------------------

Redesign required if:

- Core oscillates visibly under torsion test
- Radial braces deflect > 3 mm under static load
- Mount bolts loosen after dynamic test
- Printed clamps show creep or excessive deformation
