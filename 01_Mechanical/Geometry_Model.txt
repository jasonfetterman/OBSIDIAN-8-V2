OBSIDIAN-8 V3 – GEOMETRY MODEL
Revision: V3.3
Status: Authoritative

------------------------------------------------------------
1. CORE DIMENSIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

Standing Height (Neutral): 1143 mm
Target Total Mass: ≤ 20 kg
Crouch Height: ~680 mm CG reference
Maximum Stair Rise: 200 mm

Body Configuration:
Hybrid 3030 aluminum extrusion backbone + 2020 secondary supports, octagonal cross-section frame
3D printed modules for limbs, armor, and payload bays

------------------------------------------------------------
2. BODY STRUCTURE
------------------------------------------------------------

Frame Type:
3030 aluminum extrusion backbone forming central load paths
2020 extrusions for secondary braces and sensor/payload mounts
3D printed mounting brackets for actuators, panels, and electronics

Central Electronics Plane:
Approx. 300 mm diameter mounting plate
Material: 3 mm aluminum or 5 mm polycarbonate
Mounted to 3030 backbone via T-slot nuts

Leg Mounting:
Reinforced 3D printed brackets with embedded gussets
M6 fasteners to 3030 backbone for high-torque attachment

Structural Design Goal:
Minimize torsional flex under tripod gait
Preserve modularity for maintenance and upgrades

------------------------------------------------------------
3. LEG GEOMETRY
------------------------------------------------------------

Per Leg:
3 DOF configuration

Segments:
Coxa – printed bracket, reinforced
Femur – printed with internal ribs for torsion resistance
Tibia – printed, long for stair clearance

Design Bias:
Long tibia for stair clearance
Moderate femur for torque efficiency
Coxa width optimized for lateral stability
Reinforced attachment points to extrusion backbone

------------------------------------------------------------
4. NEUTRAL STANCE GEOMETRY
------------------------------------------------------------

Neutral Stance Requirements:

- Feet positioned for maximum stability polygon
- CG centered within 50 mm of geometric center
- No servo near stall torque at neutral
- Tilt tolerance: ≤ 5° in neutral

Stance Polygon:
Fully encloses CG projection during static posture
Backbone and printed brackets maintain alignment

------------------------------------------------------------
5. TRIPOD GAIT GEOMETRY
------------------------------------------------------------

Tripod Sets:
Set A: LF, RM, LR
Set B: RF, LM, RR

Lift Height (Normal Mode): ~80–120 mm
Lift Height (Stair Mode): ~200 mm

Stride Length:
Scaled based on voltage and performance mode

Design Requirement:
No CG excursion outside support triangle
Hybrid frame maintains rigidity to prevent leg deflection

------------------------------------------------------------
6. STAIR MODE GEOMETRY
------------------------------------------------------------

Front Tripod:
Increased lift arc to ≥ 200 mm

Body Pitch Compensation:
Forward CG shift prior to rear tripod lift

Maximum Safe Pitch:
≤ 10° sustained during stair ascent

Abort Conditions:
- Excessive tilt
- Current > threshold
- Voltage sag

------------------------------------------------------------
7. HEAD MODULE GEOMETRY
------------------------------------------------------------

Head Position:
Forward-biased mount above CG plane

Contains:
- 6 RGB cameras (wide field coverage)
- 1 depth camera
- IMU
- RTK antenna mount (top surface, clear sky exposure)

Cable Routing:
Strain-relieved through protected channels integrated in printed brackets and extrusion backbone

------------------------------------------------------------
8. CG TARGET MODEL
------------------------------------------------------------

Primary CG Target:
Within central 3030 backbone octagon

Vertical CG:
As low as practical while preserving stair clearance

Battery Placement:
Lowest practical mounting position (3D printed housing integrated with backbone)

Heavy Compute Placement:
Centralized and thermally isolated (mounted on 3030 backbone plate)

------------------------------------------------------------
9. DESIGN CONSTRAINTS
------------------------------------------------------------

- No structural member may flex enough to disturb SLAM alignment
- No servo horn shall be load-bearing without reinforcement
- Bearing-supported joints required at primary torque transfer points
- Printed components must not carry primary torsional loads
- All 3D printed brackets must interface with extrusions using M5/M6 fasteners or embedded gussets

------------------------------------------------------------
10. DESIGN PRIORITIES
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Stability under load
2. Predictable CG behavior
3. Stair safety
4. Cable protection
5. Modular serviceability
6. Hybrid integration of extrusion backbone and printed parts

------------------------------------------------------------
END OF GEOMETRY MODEL
