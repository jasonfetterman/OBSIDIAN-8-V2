------------------------------------------------------------
1. PLATFORM CLASSIFICATION
------------------------------------------------------------

Hybrid Indoor / Outdoor Autonomous Octopod Mobility Platform  

Primary Role: Persistent patrol and adaptive mobility across mixed terrain environments.  
Secondary Role: Command node for distributed swarm operations, providing navigation and energy coordination.

------------------------------------------------------------
2. PHYSICAL ENVELOPE
------------------------------------------------------------

Standing Height: 1143 mm (3.75 ft)  
Crouch Height: ≈ 680 mm CG height  
Target Mass: ≤ 20 kg  
Stair Capability: 200 mm rise  
Footprint: Octagonal stance for static stability and tripod redundancy  

Design Priority: **Stability > Endurance > Speed**

------------------------------------------------------------
3. MISSION DURATION MODEL
------------------------------------------------------------

Target Deployment Cycle: ≥ 3 hours (mixed‑mode operation)  

Typical Mission Energy Profile:  
- 40 % Active Locomotion  
- 40 % Stationary Scanning / Sentry Mode  
- 20 % Low‑Speed Adjustment / Repositioning  

Energy Reserve Rule: Maintain ≥ 15 % projected reserve for safe return to dock.  
Reference: /03_Control_Architecture/Energy_Model.txt  

------------------------------------------------------------
4. ENVIRONMENTAL CONDITIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

Indoor Operation:  
- SLAM with LiDAR + Depth Fusion  
- Stair Traversal ≤ 200 mm rise  
- Dynamic Obstacle Avoidance  
- Low‑light navigation (RGB‑Depth fusion)  

Outdoor Operation:  
- RTK‑GPS centimeter accuracy  
- Uneven terrain adaptation and classification  
- Perimeter and sector patrol  
- Wind and light rain tolerant enclosure  

Seamless transition between indoor and outdoor domains is mandatory.

Operational Temperature Range: −10 °C to +40 °C nominal.  
Humidity: 0–90 % non‑condensing.  
Reference: /10_Lifecycle/Environmental_Qualification_Plan.txt  

------------------------------------------------------------
5. TERRAIN CAPABILITIES
------------------------------------------------------------

Supported Surfaces: Concrete, Tile, Carpet, Grass, Packed Dirt, Asphalt, Standard Stairs (≤ 200 mm rise)  

Crouch Mode: Automatic for low‑clearance zones (< 700 mm headroom)  

Not Recommended: Loose gravel, mud, standing water > 25 mm.  

------------------------------------------------------------
6. PERFORMANCE MODES
------------------------------------------------------------

**Normal Mode** — Balanced stride frequency and energy consumption.  
**Aggression Mode** — Increased stride height and velocity; higher power draw.  
**Conservation Mode** — Reduced stride frequency; limited stair interaction.  

Thermal and voltage thresholds enforced through: /03_Control_Architecture/Thermal_Model.txt  
Mode transitions validated by Energy Model telemetry.  

------------------------------------------------------------
7. ENERGY ARCHITECTURE
------------------------------------------------------------

Development Configuration: 6 S LiPo system (21 – 25 V nominal).  
Deployment Configuration: 8 S LiFePO₄ Smart BMS.  

Autonomous Docking:  
- AprilTag‑based visual alignment  
- Contact verification sensors  
- CC/CV charge management profile  
- BMS telemetry handshake via CAN bus  

All charging events are logged and verified for telemetry consistency.  

------------------------------------------------------------
8. SAFETY DOMINANCE RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

Safety overrides all autonomy layers.  
Energy availability overrides mission intent.  
Remote control cannot override hardware kill‑line or energy reserve rules.  

Authority chain: Hardware Kill → Reactive Core → Mission Control → Remote Governance.  
Reference: /03_Control_Architecture/Authority_Model.txt  

------------------------------------------------------------
9. DESIGN SIGNATURE
------------------------------------------------------------

OBSIDIAN‑8 V3 embodies:  
- Distributed compute authority  
- Deterministic motion isolation  
- Hardware watchdog dominance  
- Energy‑aware autonomy  
- Persistent terrain memory  
- Swarm‑capable coordination  
- Fail‑safe neutral posture default  
- Documented and traceable system configuration
