1. PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------

Identify, classify, and mitigate risks associated with
design, operation, and lifecycle management of OBSIDIAN‑8 V3.

Each risk entry includes:
- Risk ID  
- Description  
- Severity  
- Likelihood  
- Mitigation Strategy  
- Verification Method / Test Reference  

------------------------------------------------------------
2. SEVERITY SCALE
------------------------------------------------------------

1 – Minor (no injury, minor inconvenience)  
2 – Moderate (system interruption)  
3 – Major (hardware damage possible)  
4 – Critical (injury risk or uncontrolled motion)  
5 – Catastrophic (severe injury or irreversible damage)  

------------------------------------------------------------
3. LIKELIHOOD SCALE
------------------------------------------------------------

1 – Rare  
2 – Unlikely  
3 – Possible  
4 – Likely  
5 – Frequent

5. RISK REVIEW POLICY
------------------------------------------------------------

Risk Register shall be reviewed:
- Before Phase 2 exit  
- Before scaled production release  
- After any Class A engineering change  
- After critical field incident  
- Annually for active fleet units  

Each update retains traceable link to Change Request record.  

------------------------------------------------------------
6. ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA
------------------------------------------------------------

No catastrophic (Severity 5) risk may remain without documented mitigation and validation.  
All mitigations must map to validated tests and auditable protocols.  

Risks shall be traceable to:
- Safety Architecture  
- System Requirements  
- Test Protocols  
- FMEA entries with matching IDs  

------------------------------------------------------------
7. DESIGN INTENT
------------------------------------------------------------

The Risk Register formalizes hazard awareness,
tracks mitigation through verification,
and reinforces continuous safety ownership.

Risk management is a live discipline maintained throughout design, production,
and field operation of OBSIDIAN‑8.
