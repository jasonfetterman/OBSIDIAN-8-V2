# OBSIDIAN-8 CABLE ROUTING STANDARD (REV A)

## Internal Routing
- All parts must include internal cable channels
- Channel width: 6–10 mm
- Use rounded edges (no sharp corners)

## Pass-Through Holes
- Between segments: 10–15 mm diameter

## Strain Relief
- Add anchor points every 100–150 mm
- Use zip-tie slots or clip points

## Connector Clearance
- XT60: minimum 16 mm space
- JST: minimum 8–10 mm space

## Routing Rules
- Avoid tight bends
- Keep wires away from moving joints
- Protect wires from pinch points
