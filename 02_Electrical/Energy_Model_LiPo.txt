1. PURPOSE

Define the energy storage model for OBSIDIAN-8 V3 using LiPo (Lithium Polymer) batteries.

Supports high-power missions requiring short bursts of current.
Integrates with reactive safety, actuators, and mass/CG calculations.
Provides reference for FMEA, deployment profiles, and CM documentation.
2. BATTERY SPECIFICATIONS
Parameter	Value / Range	Notes
Chemistry	LiPo	High discharge capability
Nominal Voltage	11.1 V	3 cells in series
Capacity	10 Ah	Per module
Max Continuous Discharge	20 A	For sustained operation
Peak Discharge	50 A	Short bursts < 5 s
Charge Voltage	12.6 V	Constant voltage charge
Charge Current	5 A	CC-CV charging recommended
Depth of Discharge (DoD)	80%	Maintain longevity
Cycle Life	~500 cycles	At 80% DoD
3. ENERGY MANAGEMENT MODEL
State-of-Charge (SOC) Calculation:
SOC (%) = (Remaining Capacity / Total Capacity) × 100
SOC updated via integrated BMS telemetry in real-time.
Voltage Modeling:
Open-circuit voltage (OCV) mapped against SOC.
Load-dependent voltage drop calculated to prevent actuator undervoltage.
Thermal Considerations:
Temperature monitoring required (max 60°C).
Output derated above 50°C for safety.
Overcurrent / Undervoltage Protection:
BMS limits maximum draw based on continuous and peak ratings.
System triggers safe shutdown if voltage drops below 9.0 V per module.
4. DEPLOYMENT INTEGRATION
Module IDs and placement recorded in deployment_profile.
SOC limits integrated with FMEA:
FM-003 (undervoltage during movement) triggers pre-cutoff stop.
Telemetry allows predictive maintenance and load balancing between modules.
5. CONTROL INTERFACES
Actuator Power Management:
Limits maximum torque based on available energy.
Prevents simultaneous high-current draw across all actuators exceeding BMS limits.
Reactive Safety Layer:
Overrides mission compute commands if SOC drops below minimum safe threshold (20%).
Ensures predictable and safe behavior.
Charging / Swapping:
Hot-swap capable with automatic SOC recalibration.
Follows manufacturer-recommended CC-CV charging profile.
6. VALIDATION AND TESTING
SOC calibration verified with controlled discharge tests.
Voltage response tested under peak load conditions.
Thermal performance validated from -20°C to 60°C.
Integration tests conducted with Mass_and_CG_Model, Structural_Load_Model, and actuator systems.
7. DESIGN INTENT
Provide high-power energy source for demanding missions.
Maintain safe and predictable operation under all conditions.
Fully compatible with CM, FMEA, deployment profiles, and control authority hierarchy.
Enable energy-aware actuation and safe motion under dynamic load conditions.
